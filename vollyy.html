Volleyball — My Favourite Hobby

Fast, fun, team sport.

Introduction

Volleyball is a team sport where two teams are separated by a net and try to score points by grounding the ball on the opponent's court.

Fact: Volleyball was invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan.

Photos

Basic Rules

  1. Six players per team.
  2. Each team may touch the ball up to three times before sending it over the net.
  3. The ball must not be caught or thrown.
